Interaction Models: Bento Studio V2

Choosing how to add, edit, and delete

1

Contextual Sidebar

RECOMMENDED

The sidebar handles everything. It shows the "Widget Palette" by default. Click a card on the grid, and the sidebar switches to show that card's "Properties" (Edit/Delete).

2

Toolbar + Modal

Hover over a card to see icons. Click the trash to delete. Click the pencil to open a modal with settings.

3

Live Palette + Inline

Always drag-to-add. Click a card to edit its ID/Type directly in place on the grid.

Recommended: Contextual Sidebar
